Who I Am

I am Nuruzzaman Shawon, an Adjunct Instructor, Cybersecurity & Network Security Specialist, and Academic Researcher with over a decade of experience spanning academia, IT infrastructure, and international project management. My work bridges the gap between technology, education, and leadership, with a focus on building secure, innovative, and inclusive learning environments.

At Pacific States University, I teach and mentor students across computer science, business, and analytics while also contributing to the university's digital and cybersecurity infrastructure. My research explores machine learning, AI-driven diagnostics, and advanced cybersecurity strategies, published in peer-reviewed journals and presented in global academic forums.

Passionate about global education standards and cross-border collaboration, I focus on innovation, leadership, and integration of technology into higher education.
